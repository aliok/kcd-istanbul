Why KCD Istanbul?

Kubernetes Community Days (KCDs) are global, community-organized events that gather adopters and technologists from open source and cloud native communities for education, collaboration, and networking. KCDs are supported by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF).

After a successful KCD in 2023 , we are excited to announce KCD Istanbul 2024!

Whether you're a seasoned developer, a curious beginner, or a business leader navigating the cloud, our conference offers a unique platform to connect, learn, and see the current state and the future of cloud native technologies.

What to Expect

  • Insightful Keynotes: Gain valuable perspectives from industry leaders shaping the cloud native ecosystem.
  • Deep-dive talks: Learn from experts about cloud native technologies.
  • Interactive Workshops: Dive deep into hands-on sessions.
  • Community Collaboration: Forge connections with like-minded individuals, sharing experiences and best practices.
