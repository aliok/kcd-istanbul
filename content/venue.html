Venue

Our event will be held at Istanbul Technical University.

Istanbul Technical University is one of the world's oldest technical universities with a long history of 250 years. ITU campus is located in the heart of Maslak, a modern commercial and financial district of Istanbul.

The campus is 45 minutes away from Istanbul Airport and 50 minutes away from Sabiha Gokcen International Airport.

Getting to Istanbul

Istanbul has two international airports: Istanbul International Airport (IST) and Sabiha Gokcen International Airport (SAW). Both airports are well-connected to the city center.

Istanbul International Airport (IST) is the main airport of Istanbul and is located on the European side of the city. It is 42 km away from the venue.
Sabiha Gokcen International Airport (SAW) is located on the Asian side of the city. It is 52 km away from the venue.

Istanbul International Airport is the 2nd most connected airport in Europe and one of the top 10 most connected airports in the world, where you can find direct flights from many cities around the world.
