Getting to Istanbul

Istanbul has two international airports: Sabiha Gokcen International Airport (SAW) and Istanbul International Airport (IST). Both airports are well connected to the city center.
Istanbul International Airport (IST) is the main airport of Istanbul and is located on the European side of the city. It is 40 km away from the city center and 80 km away from the venue.
Sabiha Gokcen International Airport (SAW) is located on the Asian side of the city. It is 12 km away from the venue.
Istanbul International Airport is the 2nd most connected airport in Europe and one of the top 10 most connected airports in the world, where you can find direct flights from many cities around the world.
